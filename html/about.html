Our Story

Why We Roast

Three years ago, the founders of Press + Pour, Jenny and Donald Sweet, started this small local cafe as a way to bring people together. We believe in great coffee and good conversations. At Press + Pour, we thoroughly research the best coffee beans and roast the beans onsite. Through excellent coffee, we strive to create an atmosphere that welcomes long conversations, a cozy place to sit, or a good atmosphere to catch up with an old friend. As coffee lovers ourselves, we strive to provide the best roast. Here at Press + Pour, we hope to serve you the best coffee in a friendly, comfortable environment.
